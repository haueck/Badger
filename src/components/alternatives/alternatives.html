You can only use slash / and brackets () in this field in the way that is explained below.

You can use / to separate alternative parts in a sentence.
In this riddle
stick up for sb/oneself/sth
These three answers are correct:
stick up for sb
stick up for oneself
stick up for sth

You may use brackets to group words
bend on sth/(doing sth)
Correct answers:
bend on sth
bend on doing sth

Brackets are also used to indicate optional parts
bludgeon sb into (doing) sth
Correct answers:
bludgeon sb into sth
bludgeon sb into doing sth

Complex example with brackets and slashes
conjure sth up/((up) from/(out of) sth)
Correct answers:
conjure sth up
conjure sth from sth
conjure sth up from sth
conjure sth out of sth
conjure sth up out of sth
